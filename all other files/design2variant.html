NFT stands for "Non-Fungible Token" and is the technical term for an unique, one of a kind digital token.

The NFT is stored on the crypto blockchain called "Ethereum", and is self-governed by a 'smart contract'.

A crypto wallet is required to stored the NFT.

We recommend Metamask wallet (Chrome Browser extension - metamask.io).
